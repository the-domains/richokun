Rich Okun - Vignettes 

What we call ’I’ is just a swinging door, which moves when we inhale and when we exhale. - Shunryu Suzuki

In the chill of the morning, my breath takes form. A growing mist adorning, like fog ahead of a storm.  With my heated exhalations, there's movement and growth. Cooling, crystal accumulations whisper a frozen oath. Commanding time to stand still, as the cold etches and constructs, a perfect geometry until, the sun's radiance destructs. Inspiring extreme ambitions, when the invisible is seen, and under the right conditions reveals the magic of the in-between.
