advantages

It is the greatest of all advantages to enjoy no advantage at all. - Henry David Thoreau Some, place great importance in having the upper hand. always needing to advance, and having to be in command. But there is something to be said, and the greater to be gained, to not be so far ahead, rather be equal and contained. A great humility is invited, when equanimity is highlighted. In receptive presence of guided wisdom, a perceptive resonance to express your 'ism'.
