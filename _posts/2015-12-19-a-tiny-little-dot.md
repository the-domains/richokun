Were it not, 

for my utter 
humanness, 

I, 
a tiny little dot, 

in this infinitely 
vast universe, 

could think like God. 

In unbounded creation, 

I could drop the facade, 

and in deep meditation 

upon reality's stone, 

strike the rod
of imagination 

right into 
utter formation.
